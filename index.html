FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Will I get punched in the face my first day? +

Absolutely not. Our beginner and fitness classes involve zero hard sparring. You'll hit heavy bags, mitts, and focus on mechanics and cardio. Sparring is strictly for advanced, willing participants.

I'm totally out of shape. Am I too far gone for this? +

No. There are 200+ athletes trained here—from professionals to people who haven't worked out in 10 years. You scale the intensity to your current fitness level. We are here to push you, not break you.

Do I need any previous boxing experience? +

Not at all. The majority of our members started with zero experience. Our coaches break down the fundamentals step-by-step so you learn proper mechanics safely from day one. We welcome everyone from absolute beginners to active competitors.

Is this real boxing or just a fitness class? +

It's real boxing that gets you incredibly fit. You won't find aerobic cardio-kickboxing here. You will learn authentic striking, defense, and footwork used by competitive athletes, which naturally provides an elite, stress-relieving, total-body workout.

Do I need to buy expensive gear before my trial? +

Nope. Just bring workout clothes, a water bottle, and a good attitude. We have loaner gloves available for your first week.
