FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Will I get punched in the face my first day? +

Absolutely not. Our beginner and fitness classes involve zero hard sparring. You'll hit heavy bags, mitts, and focus on mechanics and cardio. Sparring is strictly for advanced, willing participants.

I'm totally out of shape. Am I too far gone for this? +

No. We've trained over 200+ individuals—from athletes to people who haven't worked out in 10 years. You scale the intensity to your current fitness level. We are here to push you, not break you.

Do I need to buy expensive gear before my trial? +

Nope. Just bring workout clothes, a water bottle, and a good attitude. We have loaner gloves available for your first week.
