Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any previous boxing experience? +

Not at all. The majority of our members started with zero experience. We break down the fundamentals step-by-step so you learn safely from day one.

Will I get punched in the face my first day? +

Absolutely not. Our beginner and fitness protocols involve zero hard sparring. You'll hit heavy bags and mitts to build your conditioning. Sparring is strictly for advanced, willing participants.

Do I need to buy expensive gear before my trial? +

Nope. Just bring workout clothes, a water bottle, and a good attitude. We have loaner gloves available for your first week.
