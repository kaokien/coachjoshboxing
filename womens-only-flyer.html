Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any previous boxing experience? +

Not at all! This class is designed to be 100% beginner-friendly. Coach Josh will teach you the fundamentals of striking, stance, and defense step-by-step.

Will I be sparring or getting hit? +

No, there is zero sparring in this class. You will get an intense, stress-relieving workout by hitting the heavy bags and focus mitts in a completely safe, non-intimidating environment.

Do I need to buy expensive gear before the trial? +

Nope! Just wear comfortable workout clothes and bring a water bottle. We have loaner boxing gloves ready for you when you walk in.
