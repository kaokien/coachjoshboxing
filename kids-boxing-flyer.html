Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for my child? Will they get hit in the head? +

Absolutely safe. There is zero head contact or hard sparring in the youth classes. They focus on hitting heavy bags, focus mitts, and learning proper technique and defense in a highly controlled environment.

Do I need to buy expensive gear before the trial? +

Nope! Just have them wear comfortable athletic clothes and bring a water bottle. We have clean loaner gloves available for their entire 7-day trial period.

What age group is this for? +

This specific class is for children ages 6 to 12. If your child is 13 or older, they will join the Teens & Adults classes which run later in the evening.
